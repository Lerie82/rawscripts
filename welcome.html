About

What is RawScripts?
RawScripts is online screenplay editing software that syncs all your scripts to Google Docs. Because they're all online, you can get to them anywhere you have an internet connection.
Seems dumb. What are the benifits?
Your files arn't on one computer, they're anywhere you want them. Not at your home computer? Laptop died? Visiting the folks for a week and forgot to send yourself your latest revisions? Doens't matter. Everything is online, accessible from any browser. Heck, just met an actor that would be great for your next project? Email him the script from your smartphone.
How do I login?
All you need is a Google Account. If you don't have one, you can sign up for one here. Once you have one, all you need to do is authenticate your account to work with RawScripts.
Browsers
Just right quick, we recommend you use the latest version of FireFox, Safari, or Chrome. It'll work in older versions, it'll work in other browsers, just recomending for now you use one of those three.

Security and Privacy

What's this Authentication? Seems sketchy.
We save all your scripts to Google Docs, but to get in there, we need your permission. So if this is your first time loging in, you'll be taken to a Google login page, you'll go through a little process to allow or deny access.

If you agree to everything, Google will send you back to RawScripts, and you're good to go. RawScripts will get your Email (so we know who your are when you login) and some secure Google code (so we can update scripts in your Docs).
What information does RawScripts have on me?
Not much, really. We don't have your password, we can't access your other google products, we don't know you by anything but your email address. We don't even store your scripts. All of your data is stored with Google and you allow us to access it when you need it.
