RawScripts is online screenwriting software. Scripts are private, secure and accessible from any computer. Nothing to install. You can try out the application, or login with your Google or Yahoo account and get working on your screenplay.

No New Usernames and Passwords

We try to make it simple. If you have a Google or Yahoo account, just click the button, and sign in. No new names, no registering, just start writing.

All in the Browser

Nothing to download, nothing to install. Just login and start typing in seconds. And because everything is online, you have your scripts, your notes, and a complete draft history from any computer. Your home desktop, your travel laptop, even your smartphone.

Simple Import/Export

Have scripts started in different software? Easily upload them with formats preserved. You can import all your old scripts in seconds.

Done with RawScripts? We don't want to keep your files hostage. We make it very easy to get your scripts out and back into Final Draft, Celtx, or any other screenwriting software.
